Directory

In order to navigate through the application, you can use the navigation bar located on the left side of the screen. The navigation bar contains three icons: a home icon, a template icon, and a contact icon. The home icon will take you back to the home page, the template icon will take you to the template dashboard, and the contact icon will take you to the contact page.

Your Templates

The "Your Templates" page is where you can view all of the templates that you have created. You can also create new templates, edit existing templates, delete templates, and download templates from this page.

Creating a Template

To create a new template, click on the "Create" button located on the "Your Templates" page. This will open a new template editor where you can enter your message and customize the template to your liking.

Here you will be asked to enter a title for your template, as well as the message you would like to save.If you have certain attributes or plugins you would like to be able to input into your message for specific use cases, you can add them here by entering the type of attribute surrounded by square brackets like so: [company name] or [first name] or [date]. This will allow you to input these attributes when you use the template in the future.

After you are finished with all of the required information, you may click the "submit" button to save your template. You will then be redirected back to the "Your Templates" page where you can view your newly created template.

Editing a Template

To edit an existing template, click on the "Edit Template" button located underneath the template you would like to edit on the "Your Templates" page. This will open the template editor where you can make changes to the message, name, and attributes of the template.

After you are finished with all of the required information, you may click the "update" button to save your changes. You will then be redirected back to the "Your Templates" page where you can view your updated template.

Deleting a Template

To delete a template, click on the "Delete Template" button located underneath the template you would like to delete on the "Your Templates" page. This will prompt you to confirm that you would like to delete the template. If you confirm, the template will be deleted and you will be redirected back to the "Your Templates" page.

Downloading a Template

To download a template, click on the "Download Template" button located underneath the template you would like to download on the "Your Templates" page. This will prompt you to download the template as a .PDF file. You can then save the file to your computer or device.

An alternative to downloading the template is to click the "copy" button located underneath the template. This will copy the template to your clipboard, allowing you to paste it into an email, document, or other application.

Viewing/Using a Template

To view or use a template, simply click the card with the correct name of the template you would like to view. This will open the template in a new text area where you can view the message and attributes of the template.

If you have changeable attributes, you will find all of them located above the text area. Here you can input the correct information for each attribute and either click enter, or click outside of the input box to update the message with the correct information.

Afterwards you can do with the message what you please. You can copy the message to your clipboard, download the message as a PDF, or simply view the message in the text area.
